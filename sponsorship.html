Why Sponsor Us

Sponsors enable us to build better vehicles, travel to competitions, and focus on research. In return, we offer visibility, talent pipeline, and alignment with cutting-edge autonomous systems and mobility.

Partnership Tiers

Supporting

Recognition on our website and materials. Ideal for companies and individuals who want to support student engineering.

Partner

Logo placement, social mentions, and optional tech or in-kind support. Great for industry partners in autonomy and mobility.

Title / Premier

Premium visibility, direct collaboration on projects, and priority recruitment access. For organizations making a major commitment.
