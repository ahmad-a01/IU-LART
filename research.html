Research

Our work spans perception, planning, control, and systems integration for autonomous racing and mobility.

Research Areas

Perception

Sensor fusion, object detection, tracking, and state estimation for high-speed and dynamic environments.

Planning & Control

Path planning, trajectory optimization, model predictive control, and real-time decision-making on the track.

Simulation & Validation

Simulation pipelines, scenario generation, and validation methods to accelerate development safely.

Publications & Open Source

We aim to publish and share our findings where appropriate and contribute to open-source tools that benefit the autonomous systems community. Details will be added as work is released.
